Who We Are

SCM solicitors were formed in January 2015 with the merger of Bridgend firms David Sedgwick Solicitors and Chudleigh Solicitors. The merger brought together 2 specialist criminal practices to become one of South Wales leading criminal law firms. We are a specialist criminal practice which services clients across the length of South Wales. Our experienced team of solicitors offer our clients a tailored service to meet their individual needs.
